## Datacenter vs Residential: Which Estonia Proxy Type Should You Pick?

This is where most buyers stall. The choice isn't always obvious, and picking wrong wastes money.

**Datacenter proxies** live in commercial server farms. They're fast, cheap, and easy to provision in bulk. Sites that don't aggressively detect proxies — most basic websites, internal tools, low-stakes scraping — handle them fine. The downside: anti-bot systems on major retailers, banks, and social platforms can spot datacenter IPs in miliseconds.

**Residential proxies** route through real homes and mobile devices via consumer ISPs. To the destination server, your traffic looks identical to a regular Estonian household browsing the web. The tradeoff is bandwidth-based pricing and slightly slower response times.

A simple decision rule:

- Scraping public data, doing SEO checks, monitoring ads → **datacenter is fine**
- Managing social accounts, scraping retailers, sneaker drops, financial services → **residential is the safer call**
- Long-running sessions where the IP must stay consistent → **static residential orISP proxies**
